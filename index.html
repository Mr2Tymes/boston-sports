Key moments, top performances, and game recaps from the 2025 Boston sports season.

Patriots · Week 17

Maye Goes 90% vs. Jets

Drake Maye completed 19 of 21 passes for 256 yards and a career-high five touchdowns in a 42–10 blowout win over the Jets, clinching the AFC East title. His 99.8 QBR was the highest ever recorded in the stat's history.

Red Sox · 2025 Season

Crochet Leads MLB in K's

Garrett Crochet became the first pitcher to finish a season with 250+ strikeouts and an ERA below 2.75 since 2019, posting a dominant 18–5 record and 2.59 ERA across 205.1 innings in his first season with Boston.

Patriots · Playoffs

Patriots Reach Super Bowl LX

The Patriots defeated the Denver Broncos 10–7 in the AFC Championship to reach their 12th Super Bowl appearance. CB Christian Gonzalez's game-sealing interception secured the win and sent New England to Super Bowl LX.
